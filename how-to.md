## Logger:
Enter your log in the text box and press on the log button to log it, Click on the export button to export the log as a txt file,

## ToDo:
You can click the "check" mark to complete the task and "delete" icon to remove them from the database.

## Timer:
- Change the values in the form in the format of hour:minute:second (h:m:s) and click on the "change" button to set the value in the timer
- Click the "play" icon to start the timer
- Click the "pause" icon to pause the timer
- Click the "change" button to reset the timer
